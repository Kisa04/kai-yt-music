PLAYER
  • Arrow Up: increase volume
  • Arrow Down: decrease volume
  • Center/Enter: play/Pause
  • Button 2: increase playback rate by +0.25
  • Button 5: reset playback rate to 1
  • Button 8: decrease playback rate by -0.25
MAIN PLAYER
  • Arrow Left: play previous track
  • Arrow Right: play next track
  • Button 1: rewind -10s
  • Button 3: skip forward +10s
  • Button *: toggle repeat
  • Button #: toggle shuffle
MINI PLAYER
  • Arrow Left: rewind -10s
  • Arrow Right: skip forward +10s
